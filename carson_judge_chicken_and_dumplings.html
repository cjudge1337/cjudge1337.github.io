Chicken and Dumplings

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

A recipe for an easy-to-make, yet delicious homemade chicken and dumplings dish.

The wikipedia page with more info.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 2 tsp shortening
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • Lots of chicken

Directions

  1. Make broth of chicken, parsley, celery, bay leaves - about 2 inches deep.
  2. Add milk and flour for a gravy.
  3. Spoon dumplings in.
  4. Cover for 15 minutes. DO NOT OPEN UNTIL DONE.
